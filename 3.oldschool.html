OLD SCHOOL

Original by Norman Collins, the Old School style works with primary colors (blue, yellow and red) in addition, of course, to thick borders in black tones.

In general, their colors are strong and striking, and they generally do not mix. They refer, in some cases, to the navy, according to Collins' historical background.
